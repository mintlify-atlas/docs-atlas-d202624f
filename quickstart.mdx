Welcome to Scira, your agentic research platform that plans, retrieves, and cites — so you can think faster.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Head to [scira.ai](https://scira.ai) and sign up with your preferred method:
    
    - GitHub
    - Google
    - Twitter/X
    
    No credit card required to get started. Free users get 10 daily searches and 5 extreme searches.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a search mode">
    Scira offers 17 specialized search modes to match your research needs:
    
    - **Web** — AI-powered analysis across the entire web
    - **Academic** — Research papers and scholarly sources
    - **X** — Real-time posts, trends, and conversations
    - **Reddit** — Community discussions and insights
    - **GitHub** — Repositories and developer discussions
    - **Stocks** — Market data and financial analysis
    - **Crypto** — Cryptocurrency research via CoinGecko
    - **YouTube** — Video summaries and transcripts
    - **Extreme** — Deep multi-step research with analysis
    
    Don't worry about choosing — Scira can auto-select the best mode based on your question.
    
    <Tip>
      Start with **Web** mode for general questions, or **Extreme** mode when you need comprehensive research with multiple sources.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask your first question">
    Type your question naturally and hit enter. Scira will:
    
    1. Break down your question into search queries
    2. Search across multiple sources in parallel
    3. Cross-check evidence and verify information
    4. Return a grounded answer with inline citations
    
    **Example questions to try:**
    ```
    What are the latest developments in AI reasoning models?
    ```
    
    ```
    Compare the performance of GPT-4 vs Claude Sonnet
    ```
    
    ```
    What's the current sentiment on Tesla stock?
    ```
    
    Every fact in your answer will have a clickable citation — click any source to verify the evidence yourself.
    
    <Note>
      Scira uses multiple search providers (Exa, Firecrawl, Parallel, Tavily) to ensure comprehensive coverage and accuracy.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add context with files or URLs">
    Make your research more targeted by providing context:
    
    **Upload a PDF:**
    - Click the attachment icon
    - Select your PDF file
    - Ask questions about the content
    
    **Paste a URL:**
    - Include any URL in your question
    - Scira extracts content automatically
    - Supports tweets, YouTube videos, TikTok, Instagram, and more
    
    **Supported file formats:**
    - PDF documents
    - CSV spreadsheets
    - DOCX files
    - Excel files
    
    <Tip>
      For technical PDFs, try: "Summarize the key findings in this research paper" with your PDF attached.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore advanced features">
    Once you're comfortable with basic searches, unlock more power:
    
    ### Lookouts (Scheduled Research)
    Set up recurring research agents that monitor topics and email you updates:
    ```typescript
    // Lookouts run on your schedule
    schedule: "daily" | "weekly" | "monthly"
    ```
    
    ### Connectors (Pro)
    Search your connected knowledge bases:
    - Google Drive
    - Notion workspaces
    - OneDrive files
    
    ### Memory (Pro)
    Build a personal memory companion that remembers context across conversations:
    ```typescript
    // Save important findings
    add_memory({ content: "Key insight" })
    
    // Search your memories
    search_memories({ query: "previous research" })
    ```
    
    ### Voice Mode (Pro)
    Conversational AI with real-time voice interaction for hands-free research.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Master the workflow">
    **Best practices for powerful research:**
    
    1. **Be specific** — Include dates, metrics, and technical terms
       - ❌ "AI news"
       - ✅ "Latest AI reasoning models released in 2026"
    
    2. **Use the right mode** — Match the mode to your question type
       - Financial questions → **Stocks** or **Crypto** mode
       - Academic research → **Academic** mode
       - Social sentiment → **X** or **Reddit** mode
    
    3. **Verify sources** — Click citations to audit evidence
       - Every answer includes inline citations
       - Sources show favicon, title, and URL
       - Cross-check multiple sources for accuracy
    
    4. **Iterate and refine** — Ask follow-up questions
       - Build on previous answers
       - Request deeper analysis
       - Ask for specific data points
    
    <Note>
      Scira's agentic planning means it can break complex questions into multi-step workflows automatically. For deep research, use **Extreme** mode.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
