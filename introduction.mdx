  <Steps>
    <Step title="Visit Scira">
      Go to [scira.ai](https://scira.ai) and create an account. Sign up with GitHub, Google, or Twitter for instant access.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Choose your search mode">
      Select from 17 specialized search modes based on your research needs:
      - **Web** for general internet searches
      - **Academic** for research papers and scholarly sources
      - **X** for real-time social media insights
      - **Code** for programming documentation and examples
      - **Extreme** for deep multi-source research
      
      Or let Scira automatically select the best mode for your query.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Ask your question">
      Type your question naturally. Scira's agentic planning will break complex queries into steps, search multiple sources, and synthesize a grounded answer with inline citations.
      
      <Accordion title="Example response">
        Every answer includes:
        - **Cited sources** you can click to verify
        - **Multi-step reasoning** showing how Scira researched your question
        - **Follow-up suggestions** to continue exploring
      </Accordion>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Explore advanced features">
      - Upload PDFs or paste URLs for context-aware search
      - Schedule **Lookouts** for recurring research that emails you updates
      - Connect Google Drive, Notion, or OneDrive to search your own content
      - Enable **Memory** to build a personal knowledge base
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
